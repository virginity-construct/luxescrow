Escrow Options

Crypto (USDT via MetaMask)

Secure, anonymous transactions using USDT through MetaMask wallet.

  1. Client deposits USDT into escrow wallet
  2. Funds are held securely until service completion
  3. Upon confirmation, funds are instantly released to provider

Beem to @RTGS

Fast and reliable local transfers through Beem.

  1. Client sends funds via Beem to our @RTGS account
  2. Funds are held in escrow until service completion
  3. Provider receives payment immediately after confirmation

Cash Deposits

Discreet cash deposit options for maximum privacy.

  1. Client makes cash deposit at designated location
  2. Deposit is verified and held in escrow
  3. Cash is transferred to provider after service completion
