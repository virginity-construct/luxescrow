Frequently Asked Questions

How does the escrow process work?

Our escrow process is simple: the client places funds in escrow, the service provider is notified, the service is delivered, and then funds are released. This ensures security for both parties.

How do I know my information is secure?

We employ military-grade encryption for all data, maintain strict access controls, and follow a minimal data retention policy. Your privacy is our top priority.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept USDT cryptocurrency (ERC-20 and TRC-20), Beem transfers to our @RTGS account, and cash deposits via CBA SmartATM.

How long does verification take?

Most verifications are completed within 24 hours. For expedited verification, contact our concierge team.

What happens if there's a dispute?

Our mediation team will review the case, gather information from both parties, and make a fair determination based on the evidence provided.

Are there any fees?

Yes, we charge a 5% escrow fee on all transactions. There are no additional fees for different payment methods.

How quickly are funds released?

Upon confirmation of service completion, funds are released instantly to the provider's preferred payout method.

Is my identity protected?

Absolutely. We maintain strict confidentiality and never share your personal information with any third parties.
